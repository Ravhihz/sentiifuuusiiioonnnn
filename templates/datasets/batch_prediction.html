What is Batch Prediction?

Batch prediction applies your trained model to all unlabeled reviews in the dataset at once. This is useful for:

  • Complete Dataset Analysis: Get sentiment predictions for all reviews
  • Time Saving: Automatically label thousands of reviews in seconds
  • Quality Check: Compare predictions with manual labels to validate model
